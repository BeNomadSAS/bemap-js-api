Container resize & refresh()

Side panel

Toggling this changes the map's width. With auto-refresh ON the map re-fits; turn it OFF, switch to Leaflet, and toggle — Leaflet leaves a stale/blank strip until you press refresh().

OpenLayers and MapLibre also observe their container internally, so they adapt even without an explicit call — but refresh() is the uniform way to force it.

Bottom panel — toggling this changes the map's height (like collapsing a timeline / detail drawer). Watch the map fill the freed space. On MapLibre, the freed strip fills with tiles (not grey) — that's the resize → tile-reload fix.
